curious.image

For space enthusiasts, curious.image is the place to go to visually explore the planet Mars. Our goal was to simplify access to the countless photos taken by the Mars rover Curiosity and make them more accessible. Enthusiasm for space was the spark for this idea. The images used in the galleries are official photographs taken by NASA's rovers, which have been conducting invaluable research on Mars for years.

Tech Stack

  • Next.js
  • Node.js
  • Typescript

Notice

This is a study project and was realized within 7 days. The website is no longer live due to its experimental nature and the not inconsiderable server costs.
