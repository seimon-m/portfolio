ahoi

Since my earliest childhood, I have been a member of the Scouts, and I associate an incredible amount of my experiences with it. I am still involved in the Scouts, but now more in the background. For many years we have had a community magazine, namely ahoi. This has not been changed for years and was printed in black and white. I have done a complete redesign and changed the process to printing in color. For this I developed a design system that should appeal to children, teenagers and adults. The ahoi is published twice a year and is published and edited by myself.
