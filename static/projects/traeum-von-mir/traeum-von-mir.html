Dream of Me

What happens to your wastewater when you press the flush button? In our web project, the path your wastewater takes when it leaves your home is shown on a map. The path runs from your house, through the sewage system, to the sewage treatment plant and finally into the river Reuss. We have illustrated and described the process in the wastewater treatment plant so that you understand how your wastewater is treated.
